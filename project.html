Project Overview

Project highlights

Discover our expansive 14-acre residential community at Radha Homes, featuring 240 meticulously designed simplex houses that epitomize modern living. Each home spans 150 square yards (1000 square feet), thoughtfully crafted to provide both east and west-facing options, catering to your preference for sunlight and layout. Our homes blend contemporary architecture with functional design, ensuring optimal space utilization and aesthetic appeal. With premium amenities, quality construction, and a prime location offering easy access to schools, colleges, and major highways, Radha Homes is the perfect place to find your dream home and enjoy a balanced, fulfilling lifestyle.


SPECIFICATIONS

STRUCTURE

R.C.C Framed Structure with 4½ thick roof Slab. Slab height is 10`0*. All Concrete work is with ROBO sand.

STEEL

Sree Steel thickness 8-12MM (Column size Is 9" X 12").

CEMENT

OPC 53/43 Grade for concrete and PPC for brick work and plastering.

WALLS

Solid Red bricks in 1:6 (C.M.). Outside walls 9" thick, inside walls 4½".

DOORS & WINDOWS

Teak frames for main door. Internal doors modular flush with SS finish hardware. UPVC windows with safety grill.

FINISHING

Internal walls: cement plaster, sponge finish. External walls: two coat cement plaster, sponge finish.

TOILETS

Ceramic tiles up to lintel level, Antiskid tile flooring.

FLOORING

2' x 2' Vitrified tiles (40/-Rs range) with skirting in all rooms.

PARKING

1’x1’ Parking tiles in parking area.

TERRACE

Waterproof flooring, overhead water tank with 1500 ltrs capacity.

KITCHEN

Granite platform with Quartz sink, 2' height ceramic tiles.

ELECTRICAL

Concealed copper wiring, modular switches, AC points as required.

SANITARY

Jaguar or similar make fixtures, floor mounted EWC/Indian WC, provision for geyser.

PLUMBING

PVC drainage lines (SUDHAKAR/ASHIRWAD), CPVC water lines (ASHIRWAD).

PAINTING

Internal walls: Birla White Putty finish, Asian Tractor Emulsion. External walls: Ace painting.

TV & PHONE

Concealed wiring for T.V. & Phone, provision for WI-FI Router.

ELEVATION

Ground floor brick work, marble staircase steps, S.S Railing.

COMPOUND WALL

Up to plinth level pile footing with beam, 4' height cement brick wall on three sides.
